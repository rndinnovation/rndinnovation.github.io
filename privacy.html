Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Roami keeps your data on your device. No account required. We don't collect it, sell it, or track you.

What stays on your device

Everything you log in Roami — workouts, expenses, journal entries, focus sessions, and your pet's progress — is stored locally on your device. It is never sent to our servers, and we cannot read it.

What we don't collect

  • No account, name, email, or phone number
  • No location, contacts, or photos
  • No advertising or cross-app tracking
  • No selling or sharing of personal information

Deleting your data

Deleting Roami from your device removes the data it stored. Nothing is left behind with us.

Changes and contact

If we change how Roami handles information, we'll update this page and the date above.
